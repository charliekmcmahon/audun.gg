Music statistics on demand.

What if you could view your music statistics
anywhere, at any time?
Introducing TuneTrack, the first application that
enables you to do so for multiple music services.
Get on App Store

Works with Spotify, Apple Music and Last.fm!

TuneTrack comes with built-in support for the most popular music services, so your friends can use it too!
Each song on TuneTrack comes with its own data, so you can compare it to other songs easily and learn more about the song!

Quickly and easily make
custom playlists!

With the press of a button, you can create a playlist
of your top tracks and share it with friends.
Don't want to make a playlist every time?
You can just hit the share button on the bottom right
and export your top media in text format!

Multiple sorting options,
including genre and play count!

Spotify and Last.fm statistics come with filters such as time period and artists/tracks/albums, so you can get precise data for any media at any time period!
Apple Music statistics have even more precise filtering, with filters such as Date Added To Library, Last Played, and more!

Multiple themes for full customization!

TuneTrack comes with a built-in theming system and configurable app icons, so you can make the app truly personal.

What are you waiting for?

Download TuneTrack today and learn more about your music habits than you ever have before!
